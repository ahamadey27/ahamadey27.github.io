About Me

Hey there! I'm Alex Hamadey, a .NET developer based in Kingston, NY and New York City.

As I transition into software development, I bring a tenacious, creative approach to problem-solving and a commitment to continuous learning. I'm excited to apply these qualities to my new career as a junior developer.
